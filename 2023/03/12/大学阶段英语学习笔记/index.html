大学英语学习笔记

发表于 2023-03-12 更新于 2023-03-13 分类于 知识 阅读次数： 本文字数： 359 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟
### 听力 ### 阅读 ### 作文 Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay commenting on the saying “Help others, and you will be helped when you are in need.”You can cite examples to illustrate your views. You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words. Nowadays, the growing individuals are trying to help others as they firmly believe that "Help others, and you will be helped when you are in need. " This proverb reflects the truth that kindness begets kindness. To start with, helping others can bring us a sense of satisfaction and happiness. When we do something good for others, we are usually rewarded with understanding, respect, and appreciation. It also makes us feel that we are being useful and making a difference in the world. Furthermore, helping others can also be beneficial to ourselves since it is one of the best ways to strengthen relationships and make new friends. In addition, when we help others, we are more likely to receive help in return when we are in need. This is because good deeds can inspire others to reciprocate or even surpass the kindness we gave in times of difficulties. For example, I had a friend who was struggling with a medical condition. He received lots of help from his family, friends, and colleagues. It was heartwarming to witness so many people coming together to support him during this trying time. In conclusion, helping others is not only beneficial to others, but also to ourselves.It is an act of kindness that will not only be appreciated, but also repaid in the future.
